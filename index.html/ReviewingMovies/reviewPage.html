Movie Reviews

Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

When this movie first came out, I didn’t care for it because I didn’t know much about them. However, after watching it, I was impressed with the storytelling and character development. Each movie continues to be better than the last.

I will rate Guardians of the Galaxy an 8/10.

Back to the Future (1985)

In 2019, I finally watched Back to the Future. The time travel element is fascinating, and the entire cast portrayed their characters very well.

I will rate Back to the Future an 8 out of 10.

Fun fact: If you go to the O'Reilly Auto Parts website, you can find a flux capacitor among their products.

Thor: Ragnarok (2017)

By far, the best of the Thor movies. A popular criticism of the Thor franchise is that they were overall boring and attempted to be too serious for movies based on comic books. This third installment did a complete 180. It was fun, vibrant, and had amazing character development. It is widely regarded as one of the best Marvel movies, and I agree.

I will rate Thor: Ragnarok easily a 10 out of 10.
